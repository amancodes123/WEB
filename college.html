Government College of Engineering Chandrapur is established in 1996 This is the only Government Institute under Gondwana University, Gadchiroli. This Government institute is completely funded by Government of Maharashtra.


Principal's Desk
Govt. College of Engineering, Chandrapur (GCOEC) is situated in the city of a super thermal power plant and at the center of the region with abundant black-gold i.e. coal. The region contributes in fulfilling the demand of energy resource as well as power of the country while, GCOEC promises to fulfill quality technical human resource needed for these sectors in India.
